Reflection


So what did I learn from all this? Well for one data collection is not easy! I was planning on collecting data for a whole month but I'm glad I limited it to only two weeks. Although it would be a lot more insightful to have a month or a years worth of data. It's hard to observe patterns in my life with such limited data. If I could set up an easy way to collect data it might be possible to get a years worth. With that being said here's what patterns I did notice.
Doing this also made me wonder how it is people do 60-80 hour workdays! During the week I collected data I worked for only about 30 hours and I felt like any more and it would be too much.
